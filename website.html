About

I'm a joint PhD student at Aarhus University and University of York, with a background in phonetics and statistics. I am interested in how infants discover, explore and learn the speech sounds of their first language. To do this, I use techniques from acoustic phonetics, Bayesian statistics, and meta-science. If you are interested in having a chat, I'd love to hear from you!
